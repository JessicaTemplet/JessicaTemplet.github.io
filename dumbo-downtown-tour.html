Two Boroughs of Awesome!

DUMBO Bridges to Downtown Tour

Book Now!

Snap DUMBO's world-famous Manhattan Bridge selfie then walk across the Brooklyn Bridge to Downtown Manhattan. The best of two boroughs in one incredible tour.

The Journey

What You'll See

Manhattan Bridge View

DUMBO, Brooklyn

World-famous shot of Manhattan Bridge soaring between Brooklyn brownstones in the DUMBO neighborhood.

Brooklyn Bridge

Connecting Brooklyn to Manhattan

Walk to Manhattan across this architectural and artistic marvel — the world's most famous bridge.

1 Police Plaza

Downtown Manhattan

The "1PP" popularized on TV cop dramas, home of the 33,000-strong NYPD.

City Hall & Courthouses

Civic Center

Classical landmarks recognizable from countless movies and television shows.

Lower Broadway

Financial District

The "Canyon of Heroes" where athletes, astronauts and wartime's bravest were honored by NYC's famous ticker-tape parades.

Wall Street

Financial District

Center of global finance, once site of New York City's defensive fortress.

Federal Hall

Wall Street

18th-century landmark where George Washington was inaugurated as first U.S. president.

New York Stock Exchange

Wall Street

World's first, largest and most important stock exchange, with stunning neo-classical façade.

Wall Street Bull

Bowling Green

Bronze statue symbolizes American optimism and fortunes won in NYC finance. Kiss its "jewels" for good luck!

Fraunces Tavern & Museum

Broadway & Pearl Street

NYC's oldest restaurant, with dining room where Gen. Washington bid farewell to staff after American Revolution.

Staten Island Ferry / Statue of Liberty

Whitehall Terminal

Iconic ferry service between Manhattan and Staten Island, gliding right past Statue of Liberty. It's free!

The Oculus

World Trade Center

Graceful modern mall and entrance to NYC subway, built over site of tragedy.

9/11 Memorial

World Trade Center

Sitting over footprints of the north and south World Trade Center towers, with names of every victim from that day of terror.

One World Trade Center

Financial District

Tallest building in the U.S., soaring 1,776 feet tall as a reminder of American resilience, with spectacular observatory.
